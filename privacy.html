Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Summary: PhiloShorts is a content automation service. We do not collect, store, or sell any personal information from viewers or end users. This policy explains how our service operates and interacts with TikTok's platform.

1. Who We Are

PhiloShorts ("we," "us," or "our") is an AI-powered content automation platform that creates and publishes short-form philosophy video content to TikTok. Our service operates as a software tool that interacts with TikTok's Content Posting API on behalf of our own TikTok account to publish educational content.

Our website is located at https://jolly-crepe-67cd79.netlify.app/. For questions about this policy, contact us at: cagataycan@gmail.com

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Our website and service do not:

  • Collect any personal information from visitors or viewers
  • Use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics scripts
  • Require user accounts or registration of any kind
  • Store IP addresses, browser fingerprints, or device identifiers
  • Use advertising networks or third-party trackers
  • Collect email addresses or contact information from site visitors

3. Information We Collect for Service Operation

To operate our automated content publishing service, we process the following limited technical data:

  • TikTok API Tokens: OAuth access tokens issued by TikTok to authorize our account to post content. These are stored securely and used solely for API communication.
  • API Credentials: Our own developer credentials (client key, client secret) for authenticating with TikTok's API. These are never shared with third parties.
  • Content Metadata: Video titles, descriptions, and hashtags generated by AI for our own published content. No user-generated content is processed.

4. How We Use TikTok's API

Our service integrates with TikTok's Content Posting API for the sole purpose of automatically uploading AI-generated philosophy videos to our own TikTok account. Specifically:

  • We use video.publish scope to upload and publish video content to our own account only
  • We use user.info.basic scope to verify our account identity during the OAuth process
  • We do not access any TikTok user data belonging to other users
  • We do not read, store, or process any TikTok user content other than our own published videos
  • We do not perform any actions on behalf of third-party TikTok accounts

5. Data Storage and Security

The minimal operational data we hold (API tokens and credentials) is:

  • Stored as encrypted environment variables in our server infrastructure
  • Never written to logs, databases, or shared storage in plaintext
  • Accessible only to authorized system administrators
  • Rotated regularly in accordance with best security practices
  • Never sold, licensed, or transferred to any third party

6. Third-Party Services

Our service uses the following third-party APIs and services for content generation and publishing:

  • TikTok Content Posting API — for publishing videos to our account
  • Anthropic Claude API — for AI-generated video scripts and metadata
  • AI Text-to-Speech services — for audio narration of video content

These services are used solely for content production and distribution. No user personal data is shared with these services. Each service operates under its own privacy policy.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Our website is a static informational site with no interactive features, forms, or data collection mechanisms.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data from site visitors, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you believe we hold any personal data about you in error, please contact us at cagataycan@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.
